What the model does

Three ways teams use HDI Insight

Every prediction is traceable back to the exact indicators and model version that produced it.

Very High HDI

Predicting Very High Human Development

High life expectancy, strong schooling, and high GNI per capita combine into a Very High HDI classification — showing how strength across dimensions compounds.

Medium HDI

Identifying Development Gaps

Mid-range indicators for a developing nation return a Medium HDI score, surfacing exactly where healthcare, education, or income gains would move the needle most.

Low HDI

Assessing Development Intervention Needs

Lower life expectancy and limited schooling and income produce a Low HDI score, helping prioritize where policy intervention is most urgent.
